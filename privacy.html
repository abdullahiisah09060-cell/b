Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide fintech services, including your name, email, phone number, and bank account details for payouts. All document uploads for KYC are processed using end-to-end encryption.

4. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time via our support channel.
